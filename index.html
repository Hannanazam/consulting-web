Design & Consultation Services

ACE offers design support on the areas of civil and structural,. We also have the capacity to offer Design for Safety and BIM services to create projects that are safe and innovative.

Vetting and Assessment

ACE's structural engineers are well-versed in vetting and assessment of design. Our services are crucial to determining the condition of the building structure and its components, as well as ensuring public safety

Quantities & Estimation

Our team is expert in preparing estimates and we can deliver the quantity takeoffs very fast so that you don't miss the deadline.
